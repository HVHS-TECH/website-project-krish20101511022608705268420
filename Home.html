Mitsubishi Lancer Evolutions

History, performance, and rally legacy

About the Evo

The Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution, commonly known as the "Evo," is a high-performance version of the Mitsubishi Lancer. It was produced from 1992 to 2016 and became one of the most iconic rally-inspired cars ever made.

Powered by the legendary 4G63T 2.0L turbocharged engine, the Evo delivered impressive performance and all-wheel-drive control. It was designed for rally racing and gained worldwide recognition in the World Rally Championship (WRC).

Over the years, multiple versions were released, each improving performance, handling, and technology. Drivers like Tommi Mäkinen helped cement the Evo’s legacy by winning multiple championships.
