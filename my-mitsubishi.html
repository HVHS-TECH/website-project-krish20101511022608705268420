My Personal Lancer

1993 Mitsubishi Lancer GSR

This is my 1993 Mitsubishi Lancer GSR. It’s not an Evo, but it’s one trim level below it. Mine has an Evo 3 bodykit so it looks like one. It has been sitting in storage for 11 years and needs a lot of work done.

It needs a full service, fuel pump replacement, starter replacement, and possibly an engine rebuild. The interior is blue but has loose parts like the center console. The shifter bushings also need replacing because there is a lot of play in gear.

I want to repaint it a flake purple and fully restore it. It has a 4G93T 1.8L turbo inline-4 engine producing around 141kW, but mine likely makes a bit more due to mods. But i do want to swap in a 4G63T for more power and so its more like an Evo.

  • Power: im guessing around 170kw
  • Drivetrain: AWD
